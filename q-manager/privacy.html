Q-Manager (M365 Quarantine Manager for iOS) ("the app") is an iOS client for the Microsoft 365 email quarantine.

Controller

Nils Goldkuhle, In der Aue 3, 46342 Velen, Germany — email: nils@supplymarket.de (see also Legal Notice).

Important on roles: The app is an administration tool. The contents of quarantined messages (subjects, senders, recipients, headers, bodies) are personal data of your organization's Microsoft 365 tenant. For that data, your organization is the controller and Microsoft is its processor. The publisher of this app neither receives, stores nor processes that data on its own servers — it has none. The publisher is controller only for the minimal app/website data described below.

Processing

  • No data collection by the publisher. The app has no backend operated by the publisher. No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are included.
  • Data flow. The app communicates exclusively with Microsoft services: Microsoft Entra ID (login.microsoftonline.com) for sign-in and Exchange Online (outlook.office365.com) for quarantine data. Quarantine message contents are displayed on the device and are not stored permanently or transmitted elsewhere. Depending on your tenant's location, Microsoft may process this data inside or outside the EU under its own terms.
  • Local storage & legal bases. Sign-in tokens are stored in the iOS keychain (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — performing the sign-in you requested). The signed-in account name and tenant ID are stored in the app's local preferences so you can switch tenants (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in a usable multi-tenant tool). Signing out removes both.
  • Your organization. Use of the app is subject to your organization's Microsoft 365 terms; quarantine actions (release/delete) are logged by Microsoft in your tenant's audit log like any admin action.
  • Beta testing (TestFlight). If you use the app via Apple TestFlight, Apple provides the publisher with crash reports, beta usage statistics and — if you submit feedback — your feedback including your email address. See Apple's TestFlight terms for details.

Your rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability and to object (Art. 15–21 GDPR). Because the publisher holds no personal data about you, most requests are answered by pointing to the data stored locally on your device (removable via Sign out / by deleting the app) or to your organization / Microsoft for the tenant data. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR).
